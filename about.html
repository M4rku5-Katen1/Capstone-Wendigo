What
We
Do

Capstone Wendigo is a clothe brand company, and specializes in branding premanufactured clothes with our own logo and name in order to increase value. We store and ship the branded goods from our warehouse to the doorsteps of our customers. Our name and logo are a cross between the capstone of an archway and the skull of a Wendigo. It represents the darkness and unknown that lurks outside your doorway, in turn causing the customer to feel more comfortable inside their own home.

What
Makes Us
Unique

What makes Capstone Wendigo unique compared to all other clothing brand companies is that we specialize in shipping straight to the doorsteps of our customer's houses. We don't have a physical location where consumers can go to buy our goods. All of the transactions between Capstone Wendigo and its customers happen via social media or our website. Owning a piece of merchandise with our logo on it meens you support the revolution towards shipiing items and the web-based future that's coming.
