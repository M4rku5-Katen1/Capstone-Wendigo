Capstone
Wendigo


Capstone Wendigo is a clothing brand that specializes in shipping branded clothes directly to your door. What's unique is the fact that this can all be done within the comfort of your own home. By simply signing in and entering in your address, we will take care of the storing, branding, and shipping within our facility. Capstone Wendigo is perfect for the type of people who enjoy staying inside and avoiding social interaction. You can view froma wide variety of options and select the exact article of clothing you desire and we'll take care of the delivery. Studies show that more and more people prefer to order online, and since the number of people who serf the web is constantly increasing , our market grows correspondingly.

Roberto Cordova

Chairman

Roberto Cordova is the Chairman at Capstone Wendigo and is responsible for presiding over the meetings that the board of directors conduct. Past accomplishments achieved by Roberto Cordova include his influence in gaining new investors and the increase in promotion that came as a result of public speaking conferences.

Markus Katen

CEO

Markus Katen is appointed CEO of Capstone Wendigo and takes pride in making the decisions for the company in order to lead it on a successful path. Markus initially proposed the idea for the company, and the concept quickly grabbed the hearts of thousands of socially anxious people. The CEO has also had success in making changes to the variety in order to increase sales, storage, variety, and more.

Eric Villareal

General manager

Eric Villareal is our General Manager, he keeps th employees, wages, and inventory in check. Eric's job is very essential in the success of our business, since it is how our products get produced, stored, and shipped. Eric works hard to keep employees on task in order to maximize production. Inventory needs to be constantly checked in order to avoid employee theft, and keeping a responsible person like Eric on top of this keeps us in the black.
